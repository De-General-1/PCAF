What is PCAF

PCAF is a dedicated non-governmental organization committed to improving the lives of individuals with disabilities. Our mission is to empower, support, and advocate for the rights and inclusion of people with physical challenges in all aspects of society.

We have a mission to

Empower: Provide resources and training to help individuals with disabilities achieve independence and self-sufficiency.
Support: Offer emotional, financial, and practical support to individuals and families affected by disabilities.
Advocate: Promote awareness and advocate for policies that protect the rights and improve the lives of people with disabilities.

Our Vision is

empowering individuals with disabilities to lead independent, fulfilling lives. This could involve providing resources, support, and advocacy to ensure they have equal opportunities in all aspects of life.

Our Impact

500 + Individuals

Rehabilitation Success

Over the past year, we have provided rehabilitation services to over 500 individuals, helping them regain mobility and confidence. Our tailored therapy programs have enabled many to perform daily activities independently, significantly improving their quality of life.

300 + Individuals

Educational Achievements

Through our education and vocational training programs, we have empowered over 300 individuals with disabilities to acquire new skills and secure meaningful employment. These opportunities have not only provided financial independence but also fostered a sense of purpose and self-worth.

200 + wheelchairs

Assistive Devices Distribution

We have distributed more than 200 wheelchairs, prosthetics, and hearing aids to those in need, ensuring that individuals with disabilities have the tools they need to navigate their world with dignity and ease.

150 + Families

Community Support

Our support groups have become a lifeline for many families, offering a space to share experiences, find resources, and build a supportive network. These groups have helped over 150 families cope with the challenges of living with disabilities.

Advocating for Change

Accessibility Projects

We have successfully completed several projects aimed at improving accessibility in public spaces. From installing ramps and accessible restrooms to advocating for inclusive policies, our efforts have made everyday environments more navigable for individuals with disabilities.

Awareness Campaigns

Our advocacy campaigns have reached thousands, raising awareness about the rights and needs of people with disabilities. These campaigns have not only educated the public but also influenced policy changes that promote inclusivity and equality.

Transforming Communities

Since our inception, PCAF has made significant strides in improving the lives of individuals with disabilities.

Inclusive Events

We have organized numerous community events that celebrate the abilities and achievements of individuals with disabilities. These events have fostered a spirit of inclusion and have helped break down societal barriers.

Partnerships and Collaborations

By partnering with local businesses, schools, and other organizations, we have created a network of support that extends beyond our foundation. These collaborations have amplified our impact and have brought about lasting change in the communities we serve.
